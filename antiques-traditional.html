Antiques & Traditional Fireplaces

We offer a comprehensive range of antique and reproduction cast iron fireplaces, carefully selected to bring timeless elegance and character to your home. Our collection includes everything from traditional inserts and mantels to registers and baskets, each piece chosen for its quality, authenticity, and aesthetic appeal.

Our antique fireplaces are genuine period pieces, sourced from reputable dealers and restored to their former glory. For those who prefer new construction, we also offer high-quality reproductions that capture the essence of original designs with modern reliability.
