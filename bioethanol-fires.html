Bioethanol Fires

Bioethanol fires represent the perfect fusion of convenience and authenticity, combining the ease of electric fires with the beauty of a real, dancing flame. These innovative fires burn clean-burning bioethanol fuel, producing a genuine flame without the need for a chimney, flue, or gas connection.

We offer fires designed to fit into traditional fireplace inserts, as well as standalone units that can be placed virtually anywhere in your home. Bioethanol fuel produces a real flame with no smoke, soot, or harmful emissions, making it safe for indoor use without ventilation requirements. They're simple to operate and require no permanent installation.
