Electric Stoves & Media Wall Fires

Electric stoves and media wall fires are a flexible option where a chimney, flue or gas supply is not suitable. They bring flame-effect ambience to traditional fireplace openings, freestanding stove settings and contemporary media walls.

Choose from compact stove designs, inset electric fires and larger feature fires, with options for different flame effects, trims and installation styles.
