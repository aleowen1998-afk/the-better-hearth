Made to Measure gas fires

We offer gas fires made to size, which can fit almost any cast iron insert or basket. These open gas fires offer a realistic, simple to use and low-maintenance decorative flame.

Woodburning and multifuel stoves

We have a huge range of stoves that are approved for installation in smoke control areas (like London) and are made to conform to the latest regulations.

See more

Inset gas fires and stoves

Inset gas fires are a more convenient option than a traditional wood-fuelled setup, and we offer a range of options from modern glass-fronted fires to more traditional looking stoves.

See more

Electric stoves & media wall fires

Electric fires can be incorporated into a modern media wall setup or can be used as an alternative to wood or gas fires when other options aren't suitable for the space.

See more

Mantels

We have many mantels in a wide range of styles and sizes. Our most popular options are made from white marble, but we offer lots of other materials such as limestone, wood and cast iron.

Bioethanol fires

Bioethanol fires combine the convenience of electric fires with a real flame. We offer fires to go into traditional inserts as well as standalone units.
