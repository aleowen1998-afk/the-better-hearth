Inset gas fires and stoves

Inset gas fires and gas stoves offer the look and warmth of a real flame with simple controls and less day-to-day maintenance than a solid-fuel fire. We stock both modern glass-fronted fires and more traditional stove-style options.

Our team can help you match the right model to your fireplace opening, chimney or flue requirements, heat output and room style.
