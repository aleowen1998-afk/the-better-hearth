Made to Measure Gas Fires

Our made-to-measure gas fires are built to suit a wide range of cast iron inserts, baskets and fireplace openings, giving period fireplaces the convenience of gas with a natural open-flame effect.

Each fire is specified around the opening, surround and finish of your fireplace, so the result feels integrated rather than off the shelf. Browse examples below, or visit the showroom to discuss sizes, finishes and installation options.
