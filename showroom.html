Our showroom

Our showroom

We have been trading from the same premises in Islington, North London since 1976. We are a short walk from both Highbury & Islington and Holloway Road stations, and you can visit us Monday to Saturday from 10am to 5pm with no appointment needed.

What's on display

Our showroom includes working gas fires, functional stoves, antique and reproduction fireplaces, cast iron inserts, baskets, mantels and more. It is the best place to compare finishes, styles and sizes in person before choosing the right fireplace for your room.
